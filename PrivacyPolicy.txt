ALTO WEATHER — PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 6, 2026


OVERVIEW

Alto Weather is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app operates entirely on your device with no user accounts, analytics, or tracking of any kind.


LOCATION DATA

Alto Weather uses your device's location solely to fetch weather data for your current position. Your location coordinates are sent directly to weather data providers (Open-Meteo and, for US locations, the National Weather Service) to retrieve forecasts. Your location is never stored on any server we operate, and we do not maintain any record of your location history.


WEATHER DATA PROVIDERS

The app retrieves weather information from the following third-party services:

  - Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) — global weather forecasts, historical climate data, and air quality information
  - National Weather Service (weather.gov) — detailed forecasts and alerts for US locations

These services receive only the geographic coordinates needed to return weather data. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details on how they handle requests.


SAVED LOCATIONS

Any locations you add to the app are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults). This data is not transmitted to any external server.


OFFLINE CACHE

Weather data may be cached locally on your device to provide a better experience when network connectivity is limited. This cached data remains on your device and is not shared externally.


THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Alto Weather does not include any third-party analytics, advertising frameworks, or tracking SDKs. We do not use cookies, device fingerprinting, or any other identification technology.


DATA COLLECTION SUMMARY

  - Personal information collected: None
  - Usage data collected: None
  - Data shared with third parties: None
  - Advertising: None
  - User accounts: None


CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Alto Weather does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.


CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and in this document with an updated revision date.


CONTACT

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the App Store listing.
